About Me

Dr. Liao is currently a Research Scientist at UCLA Mobility Lab. He received the Ph.D. degree from the University of California, Riverside under the supervision of Dr. Matthew J. Barth and Dr. Guoyuan Wu in 2023. His Ph.D. dissertation is "A Personalized Behavior-Aware Motion Planning Framework for Intelligent Vehicle Operation". Dr. Liao is an author of 0+ refereed papers. His research achievements have been demonstrated at the Consumer Electronics Show (CES), and the best paper award by IEEE Intelligent Transportation Systems Society (IEEE ITSS) in ITSC 2024.
